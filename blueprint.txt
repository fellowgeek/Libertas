Template Commands :

[P:COMMAND]

"P" stands for current page master post.

[P:Title]
[P:Slug]
[P:Link]
[P:Image]
[P:Video]
[P:Audio]
[P:Text]
[P:Author]
[P:Description]
[P:Timestamp]
[P:Views]
[P:Tags]
[P:Keywords]
[P:Categories]


[S:TITLE|COMMAND]

"S" stands for snnipet, this is the text portion of a post which is embeded in another page (other than it's own page)

[S:Title]
[S:Title|Title]
[S:Title|Slug]
[S:Title|Link]
[S:Title|Image]
[S:Title|Video]
[S:Title|Audio]
[S:Title|Text]
[S:Title|Author]
[S:Title|Description]
[S:Title|Timestamp]
[S:Title|Views]
[S:Title|Tags]
[S:Title|Keywords]
[S:Title|Categories]


[C:COMPONENT|PARAM1=VALUE,PARAM2=VALUE,...]

"C" stands for component, this command will load a component and sets the parameters (if any)
